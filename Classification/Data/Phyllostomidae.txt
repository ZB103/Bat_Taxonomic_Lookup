*Social Structure
Some species roost alone in a single roosting site. Males live alone or with harems, while females prefer to roost with other individuals and their pups. Evidense suggests a social heirarchy with high-ranking idividuals gaining access to preferred areas of the site.
*Reproduction
Most species show no breeding season, but have one offspring at a time. Vampyrum spectrum is uniquely monogamous. Most species are seasonally polygynous.
*Seasonal Behavior
Certain species aestivate, no hibernation.
