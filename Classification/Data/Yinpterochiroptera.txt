*Suborder
This suborder consists of all bats lacking laryngeal echolocation. It is also sometimes referred to as Pteropodiformes.
Yinpterochiroptera contains the following families:
Craseonycteridae (Kitti's hog-nosed bat)
Hipposideridae (Old World leaf-nosed bats)
Megadermatidae (false vampires)
Pteropodidae (megabats)
Rhinolophidae (horseshoe bats)
Rhinopomatidae (mouse-tailed bats)
